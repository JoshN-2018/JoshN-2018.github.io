De Voorhoede

The De Voorhoede are developers who listen. They are Co-pilots for their clients as they navigate the digital world. However their current website sold them short. Working with the De Voorhoede I refreshed their brand and designed a website that was as fresh as their approach.

Brand Identity Ui design Motion Design

Positioning the De Voorhoede as a creative technical partner

From blueprint to reality. This idea is at the heart of what the De Voorhoede do. In order to communicate this to clients I looked no further than their existing logo in which a transition was already taking place.

Content you want to read

Improving the acessiblility of content was a top priority. For case studies I limited line lengths to a reading optimised 9-11 words per line. In addition I used larger type to reduce strain and generous line spacing to make each line of text easy for the reader to navigate. On mobile line spacing reduces significantly this is to counter the effect of shorter line-lengths that occur on small screens.

Sign me up

The De Voorhoede are developers who speak your language — it was important that this conversational tone come across in the website experience. To ensure this I designed a new call-to-action and form components which followed a conversational structure. This also had the added benefit of gathering essential information efficiently.

‘See how its done’ mode

The majority of work that goes into a website will never be seen by the end user. For a FE development company however we wanted to showcase the elegant solutions that front enders come up with everyday. Enter the 'see how its done' feature. With one click user can reveal the underlying features that drive the DeVoorhoede.nl experience.
