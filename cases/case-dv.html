De Voorhoede

The De Voorhoede are an international team of Front end specialists. They take the lead in all things technical building fast reliable products that are scaleable and sustainable. Their current website failed to communicate the unique character of the company and the scope of their abilities.

Brand Identity Ui design Motion Design

Positioning the De Voorhoede as a creative technical partner

The creative blueprint, gets to the heart of what the De Voorhoede do. They transform plans into functioning interactive products. As front end specialists for the blueprint ‘blue’ there could only be one choice: html color-name blue.

Case studies you want to read

Optimising the readability of DV case studies was a top priority. To achieve this we selected a reading optimised line length of 9-11 words per line. Using larger than normal type to optimise reading ease and reduce strain and generous line spacing to make each line of text easy for the reader to navigate.

‘See how its done’ mode

The majority of work that goes into a website will never be seen by the end user. For a FE development company however we wanted to showcase the elegant solutions that front enders come up with everyday. Enter the 'see how its done' feature. With one click user can reveal the underlying features that drive the DeVoorhoede.nl experience.
