Oxycoin

Oxycoin came to us with an idea to build a product that would bring crypto currency to the everyman — The product would consolidate the complex steps to buy and store crypto in to a one-stop easy service. With bullet proof security.

Brand Identity Product Design Motion Design

Just enough brand

To solve this problem I created a paired-back brand identity and UI design that was simplicity itself. Positioning the brand as a direct counter to preconceptions about the complexity of cryptocurrency.

One system to rule them all

A key challenge that needed to be overcome was defining a visual language that could adapt to the needs of marketing, onboarding and the display of complex financials whilst maintaining a consistent feel.

Mobile first

It was vital that the design offer a premium mobile experience this is how we believed most uses would use it. All interactive elements were optimised to be at their best in a space critical environment even when this necessitated significant departures from larger screen designs.
