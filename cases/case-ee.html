Elkmont
Exchange

Knoxville Tennessee had been left behind in the craft beer boom. One talented brewer was determined to change this. Elkmont Exchange was born; delivering the finest beers, matched with fantastic food and a healthy dose of local pride. We worked with Elkmont to build a brand that was proud of it’s roots and drew inspiration from the Elkmont trail-head (Smoky Mountains) that inspired the name.

Brand Identity Strategy Consultation

Inspired by the land

The Brand identity was directly inspired by the Elkmont trailhead in the smokey mountains. The word mark is derived from the chisseled typography of the trail markers themselves. The supporting mark is a constant reminder to push further — to see whats over the next hill or in the next valley.

supporting mark

A comprehensive plan of attack

We delivered extensive brand guidelines documenting the strategic positioning, the story and the use of assets. The document was to help secure over $1 million USD in investment as well as become a springboard for the many creative partners that made Elkmont Exchange happen.

A brand brought to life

We had the pleasure of working directly with MHM Architects. Using our brand assets, and taking material inspiration from the land they brought our brand to life and added another dimension to the Elkmont experience.
