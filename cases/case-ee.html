Elkmont
Exchange

Knoxville Tennessee had been left behind in the craft beer boom. One talented brewer was determined to change this. Elkmont Exchange was born; delivering the finest beers, matched with fantastic food and a healthy dose of local pride. We worked with Elkmont to build a brand that was proud of it’s roots and drew inspiration from the Elkmont trail-head (Smoky Mountains) that inspired the name.

Brand Identity Strategy Consultation

Inspired by the land

The Brand identity is directly inspired by the the – smokey mountains. The word mark mimics the chisseled typography of the trail markers. The arrow is a our supporting symbol guiding us to push further over the next hill into the next valley.

Primary mark

supporting mark

A comprehensive plan

A brand brought to life

In consultation with us (Name) architects took our identity and brought it to life in three dimensions. Using the brand assets taking inspiration from the land, using local wood, and core ten steel and collaborating with local craftsmen they added another dimension to the Elkmont experience.

Its in the details

A component based design system allowed for content to be optimized at every screen size.
