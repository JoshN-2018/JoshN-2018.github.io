The challenge: Build a brand around a completely new concept for the weight-loss market. Design an app that delivers this functionality in simple easy-to-understand way without endangering the clients business model. An identity with a scientific edge

The core of the Sinque solution is their proprietary algorithm. This algorithm takes natural fluctuations into account to give an accurate picture of a persons health and their weight-loss trajectory. In developing the identity we took direct inspiration from this ground-breaking technology and the path to success it unlocks.
